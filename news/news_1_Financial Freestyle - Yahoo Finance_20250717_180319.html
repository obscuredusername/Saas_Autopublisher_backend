Understanding Interest Rates: A Double-Edged Sword

Interest rates play a pivotal role in shaping fiscal policies and investment strategies. A rise in interest rates can increase borrowing costs for consumers and businesses, subsequently slowing economic growth. Conversely, lower interest rates tend to encourage spending and investment. Yahoo Finance regularly covers changes in interest rates, examining how these shifts impact different sectors, including real estate and technology.
